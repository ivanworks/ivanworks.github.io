OUR WORK

Description: Through out a 6 week internship, interns were placed within a company where they were able to work alongside people in the industry and other fellow interns.They assisted companies both big and small in different projects ranging in size as well, and being able to learn new skills along the way. Below are examples of projects that these interns have completed within the 6 weeks with a brief description of what their project entailed. Interns worked on projects that were related to art and media which included video editing, video production, social media marketing and much more.

CreaTV

During the summer cohort of the San Jose Works program, I had the opportunity to work at CreaTv. At CreaTV I was able to create a 5 minute documentary with 2 other fellow interns. Through this experience I have learned so much more about cameras, editing, visual storytelling, flyers, and teamwork. I had a lot of fun working with other interns and bringing our idea to life.

San Jose Public Library

I worked at San Jose Public Library as a social media marketing intern. My work is to look at different marketing trends and create ad designs to help promote events at the library. This includes researching marketing strategies, analyzing engagement patterns on instagram, creating flyers for specific events.
